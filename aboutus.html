Welcome To Our Avva Ev



Our Motto

AVVA makes electric mobility solutions to millions , affordable self mobility solutions. These AVVAEVs are modern electric scooters, low priced and provide emission-free transportation. AVVA to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy.


Our Mission

To be the voice of all EV stakeholders towards a Indian EV framework enabling a Indian leadership in EV production and commercialisation and to export EVs.

To ensure Govt policies and directives leading to the research subsidies and incentives to producers, consumers and cities that will enable India to become aworld leader in terms of production of EVs and their parts as well as of EV commercialisation.

Welcome To Our Vision

Because of the huge benefits that Electric Vehicles (EVs) bring in terms of environmental protection and energy consumption, they are undoubtedly the motorised road vehicles of the future. AVVA strives for faster adoption of EVs in India.

AVVA will become a leader in their production and commercialisation to remain industrially and environmentally competitive in the automotive sector, reduce its fossil fuel dependency and reach target set by Govt of India by 2030 for EVs & CO2emissions.

India led by visionary Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has a vision for converting all vehicles in India to electric vehicles by 2030. We at AVVA follow sincerely.
